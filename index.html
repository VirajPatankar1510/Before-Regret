About BeforeRegret

Welcome to BeforeRegret, the definitive crowdsourced decision intelligence platform for relationship dilemmas. Every day, thousands of partners stand at critical relationship forks—deciding whether to marry, leave a relationship, forgive infidelity, relocate for love, or stay for children.

BeforeRegret helps you peer into the future by reading authentic, anonymous, chronological timelines written by people who faced your exact scenario years ago. By studying real outcomes, split rates, and cumulative regret curves, you can make decisions grounded in data rather than panic.

Core Platform Decision Modules

Our interactive relationship decision suite is structured into five core, helpful modules:

  • Relationship Dilemmas & Decisional Dossiers: We provide detailed outcome metrics and historical summaries for pre-seeded bottlenecks such as:
    • Stayed with boyfriend who doesn't want marriage
    • Pardoned a partner after cheating/infidelity
    • Stayed with a partner who doesn't want children
    • Relocated or moved across countries for love
    • Issued a marriage ultimatum
    • Maintained a long-distance relationship
  • The Relationship Court & Peer Jury: Users register anonymized conflict files and present evidence. Our registered citizen jurors deliberate, write analytical arguments, and cast public verdicts to establish impartial accountability.
  • Anonymous Regret Stories Registry: Authentic diary logs detailing the emotional, psychological, and financial consequences of relationship choices. Read post-decision updates written 1, 3, or 5 years after stay-or-go moments.
  • Scientific Red Flag warning system: A community-voted dating safety registry where behaviors are audited and classified into red, yellow, and green flags to alert individuals of subtle partner behaviors they shouldn't ignore.
  • Survivor Advice Boards: Collaborative boards where partners facing immediate crises submit complex questions to be answered directly by seasoned survivors who have already emerged from equivalent storms.
  • Accredited Relationship Decision Guides: Scholarly editorial guides written by clinical family therapists, mediators, and psychologists to assist with reconciliation math, ultimatum psychology, and relocation risk calculus.
